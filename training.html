Think On Your Feet

In a digitized environment characterized by rapid and continual disruption, it is critical for organizations to be agile and quickly respond to change. As a result, businesses require leaders who are not only effective interpersonally, but who can also manage increasingly complex organizations. Effective leaders today must be able to make decisions faster and lead change efficiently, while skillfully influencing in a more interconnected, collaborative landscape. Additionally, they need to foster a “digital-ready” organizational culture that embraces – rather than resists – continuous change.

Objective:
  • Understand the key challenges senior leaders face in leading highly complex organizations
  • Examine power and influence dynamics in organizations
  • Improve your Leadership values
  • Inspiring your team to be more creative and innovative.
  • Managing through contradictory forces that firms inevitably face in fast-paced environments.
Competency Addressed : Leadership, Impact & Influence, Inperpersonal skills, team building Skills, Impact & Influence,

Effective Personal Productivity

Productivity is the true measure of success. Effective Personal Productivity program is designed to accomplish just that. Through it, we empower you with skills for enhancing personal awareness, fine-tuning your efficiency, and effectively achieving your goals. The comprehensive, multi-phase program allows you to unlock new potential by establishing superior self-management.

Objective:
  • Understand your Roles and Responsibility
  • Knowing what is the Most Important to do in Life (Priority)
  • Able to Manage Time Effectively.
  • Can Handling Distraction and overcoming Procrastination.
  • Understand the benefit of Work Life Balance
Competency Addressed : Achievement orientation, organizational awareness, self control, impact and influence, time management & initiative
